I'm interested in moving to LA, what do I need to do?
+
You can find Revelation Church’s website at the link below. When deciding to move to LA, consider doing the following to ensure success.

1. Prayerfully consider your decision. Seek God to make certain He is directing you here and that your decision is not based on excitement alone.

2. Financially consider your decision. To ensure you have enough money to go around, a good rule of thumb is to spend 30% or less of your gross monthly income on rent. For example, you should earn $70,240 a year, or $5,854 per month, to comfortably afford a one bedroom apartment in Los Angeles (this does not include utilities and monthly expenses).

3. Research, Research, Research. Understand the move you are making and gather all information necessary to make an informed decision.

We hope this information is insightful. Good Luck!
